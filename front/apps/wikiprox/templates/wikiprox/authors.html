Authors

The contributors to the Densho Encyclopedia represent a diverse range of backgrounds and experiences. Many are professional scholars who have authored important books or articles that specifically cover the topics they write about here. Others are graduate students whose research focuses on some aspect of the Japanese American experience. Still others are journalists, community activists, or individuals who have played a role in telling the Japanese American story or who have been active players in that story. The roster below contains links to their bios and a list of entries they have contributed to the encyclopedia.

Editor: Brian Niiya, Content Director, Densho.
